The Problem
Chapter 1

CTV Has Reach.
The Growth Engine is Missing.

Mobile perfected CPA-based growth. Web refined attribution funnels. CTV still operates almost entirely on CPM-based awareness models — leaving publishers without a reliable engine to drive measurable user growth.

Discovery Gap

The average household actively engages with just 2–3 streaming apps. Winner-takes-all dynamics make discovery the single biggest barrier to growth for any publisher outside the top tier.

Cross-Device Breakdown

Ads reach users on mobile, but the app lives on Roku or Fire TV. Generic links, wrong storefronts, and login friction kill conversion. 61% of marketers struggle with cross-platform attribution.

Non-Clickable Ads

CTV ads lack native interactivity. QR codes deliver sub-1% engagement (Innovid). The industry is stuck paying premium CPMs with no path to measure direct response.

Performance Framework
Chapter 5

Redefining "Performance"
for the Living Room

Install-based CPA was built for mobile. CTV needs its own performance vocabulary — one that maps to how viewers actually engage in the living room.

Install + Device Activation

App downloaded AND launched on a CTV device. Not just a store install — a verified, real activation signal on the device that matters.

First Stream Completed

Viewer watches meaningful content — not just opens the app. The truest signal of engaged discovery and the start of genuine retention.

7-Day Active Viewer

Sustained engagement that signals real household adoption — the metric that drives CPM uplift, programmatic leverage, and OS algorithm ranking.

Subscription / Trial Start

High-intent actions that drive long-term revenue. Measured with household-level incremental lift against holdout groups for clean attribution.
